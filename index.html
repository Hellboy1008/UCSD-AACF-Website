Who We Are

Asian American Christian Fellowship (AACF) is a student-led Christian organization that is committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ to the campus of UCSD. We welcome people of all cultural, racial, and religious backgrounds and beliefs as we seek to learn more about God and to grow deeper in community with each other.

Why Love? "We love because he first loved us" 1 John 4:19

Our Theme

This year, we're exploring the question "Why Love?", derived from our theme verse in 1 John 4:19. During the year, we will be reflecting on these three mini-themes, one for each quarter: "Why would God love me?", "Why should I love God?", and "Why should I love others?"
