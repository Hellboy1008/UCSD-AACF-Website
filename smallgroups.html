Small Groups

We have scheduled times to meet up and study the Bible, along with grow deeper relationships with one another.
These are known as small groups. They are available every week, with some weeks offering special formats.
Some of the small groups have do not meet at the same location every week. To receive the latest updates on these, join our Facebook page or email list!
